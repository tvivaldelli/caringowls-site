How it works

Three simple steps to protect your loved one without changing their daily routine.

1

Keep their phone number

Your loved one's existing phone number is routed through our system. No new number to remember, no new device to learn.

2

Set up approved contacts

Family members add trusted contacts through a simple web dashboard. Only approved people can call or text your loved one.

3

Scammers are blocked silently

Unknown callers and texters are filtered out before they ever reach your loved one's phone. Family is notified and can review any blocked attempts.
